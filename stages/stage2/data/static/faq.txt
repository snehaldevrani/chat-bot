CityPark Premium Parking - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the opening hours?
A: Monday to Friday: 06:00–22:00. Saturday: 07:00–22:00. Sunday: 08:00–20:00. Closed on national public holidays.

Q: How much does parking cost?
A: Regular spaces: $3.00/hour, $20.00/day, $150.00/month. EV Charging spaces: $5.00/hour, $35.00/day, $250.00/month. VIP spaces: $8.00/hour, $50.00/day, $350.00/month. Handicapped spaces: $2.00/hour, $15.00/day, $100.00/month.

Q: Can I book a parking space in advance?
A: Yes! You can reserve a parking space through our chatbot. The chatbot will guide you through collecting your name, surname, car registration number, and preferred time period. An administrator will confirm your booking.

Q: What types of parking spaces are available?
A: We have Regular (43 spaces), EV Charging (5 spaces), VIP (5 spaces), and Handicapped (4 spaces). Total 57 spaces across 4 levels.

Q: Is there parking for electric vehicles?
A: Yes. We have 5 EV charging bays (3 on Level 1, 2 in Basement) with 22kW Type 2 AC chargers. Charging cables are provided. EV charging is included in the $5.00/hour rate.

Q: Do I need a disability badge for handicapped spaces?
A: Yes. A valid disability (blue) badge must be displayed to use the 4 handicapped-accessible spaces. Unauthorized use will result in a fine and possible clamping.

Q: Is there CCTV and security?
A: Yes. The facility has 24/7 CCTV with 60+ cameras covering all areas, plus on-site security personnel during all operating hours.

Q: Is there a height restriction?
A: Ground floor: no restriction for open-air section. All covered levels (Level 1, Level 2): 2.1 meters. Basement: 2.0 meters. Vehicles taller than 2.1m should use the Ground Floor open section only.

Q: Can I park a motorcycle?
A: Yes, motorcycles are welcome. They should use the designated motorcycle bays on the Ground Floor. Motorcycles are not permitted on Level 1, Level 2, or Basement.

Q: What happens if I lose my parking ticket?
A: Report it to reception immediately. A lost ticket fee of $10 will be charged. We will verify your entry time using our ANPR (number plate recognition) system.

Q: Is the parking facility accessible for wheelchair users?
A: Yes. We have ramps on all levels, low-height barrier panels, priority elevator access, tactile paving guides, and wide-bay handicapped spaces.

Q: Can I pay by card or cash?
A: Both are accepted. Automated machines on each level accept card and contactless/mobile payments. Cash payments can be made at the Ground Floor reception desk.

Q: Is overnight parking allowed?
A: Overnight parking is not permitted unless you hold a monthly pass. Vehicles remaining after closing time will be clamped. Contact us if you need extended parking.

Q: How far in advance can I book?
A: You can book up to 30 days in advance. Reservations must be made at least 30 minutes before the desired start time.

Q: Is Wi-Fi available in the facility?
A: Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the facility.

Q: Are there restrooms in the parking facility?
A: Yes. Restrooms are located on the Ground Floor near the reception desk.

Q: What payment methods are accepted for bookings?
A: Credit card, debit card, contactless payment, and mobile payment (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Monthly pass holders can pay by bank transfer.

Q: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Reservations can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the start time with no charge. Cancellations within 2 hours of the start time may incur a $5 late cancellation fee.

Q: Can I extend my reservation?
A: Yes, subject to availability. Contact our support team or update via the chatbot before your reservation ends.

Q: Is the Basement connected to the Downtown Mall?
A: Yes. The Basement level has a direct pedestrian connection to the Downtown Shopping Mall, making it very convenient for shoppers.
