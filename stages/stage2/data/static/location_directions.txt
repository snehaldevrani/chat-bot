CityPark Premium Parking - Location and Directions

Address:
CityPark Premium Parking
123 Main Street
Downtown Business District
City Center, 10001

Landmark References:
- Directly opposite the Central City Library
- 100 meters from Central Railway Station (Main Exit)
- Adjacent to the Downtown Shopping Mall (north entrance)
- 2 blocks from City Hall

Getting Here by Public Transport:

By Train:
Take any line to Central Railway Station. Use the Main Exit (not the West Exit). CityPark is a 2-minute walk straight ahead on Main Street. Look for the green CityPark signs.

By Bus:
The following bus routes stop within 50 meters of CityPark:
- Route 12: Stop "Main Street / Central Library"
- Route 27: Stop "Downtown Business District"
- Route 45A: Stop "Central Station North"
- Route 88: Stop "City Center Hub"

By Metro/Subway:
Take the Blue Line or Red Line to "City Center" station. Take Exit B. Walk 3 minutes north on Main Street.

By Car (GPS Navigation):
Enter "123 Main Street, City Center" into your navigation. The facility has a clearly marked entrance on Main Street. There is also a secondary entrance from Park Lane (the side street on the left).

Parking Entrance Details:
- Main vehicle entrance: 123 Main Street (off Main Street)
- Secondary entrance: Park Lane (accessible from the rear)
- Height clearance: 2.1 meters for all levels
- Vehicles exceeding 2.1m height should use ground-level open parking only

Nearby Attractions:
- Central City Library (across the street)
- Downtown Shopping Mall (50 meters)
- Business District Office Towers (100-300 meters)
- Central Park (5 minutes walk)
- City Museum (10 minutes walk)
