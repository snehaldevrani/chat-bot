CityPark Premium Parking - Levels and Layout

Total Capacity: 57 spaces across 4 levels

Ground Floor:
- 10 standard regular spaces (G-01 to G-10)
- 2 handicapped-accessible spaces (G-H1, G-H2) — located closest to the elevator and main entrance
- Total: 12 spaces
- Surface: Open air with roof covering
- Height clearance: Unlimited (open air section), 2.1m for covered section
- Nearest to: Main Street entrance, reception desk, elevator

Level 1:
- 15 standard regular spaces (L1-01 to L1-15)
- 3 EV charging spaces (L1-EV1, L1-EV2, L1-EV3) — equipped with Type 2 AC chargers (22kW)
- Total: 18 spaces
- Surface: Covered indoor
- Height clearance: 2.1 meters
- Nearest to: Elevator (central), stairwell A

Level 2:
- 10 standard regular spaces (L2-01 to L2-10)
- 5 VIP premium spaces (L2-VIP1 to L2-VIP5) — wider bays, dedicated lighting, nearest to elevator
- Total: 15 spaces
- Surface: Covered indoor
- Height clearance: 2.1 meters
- Features: VIP lounge access, premium lighting, security cameras at every bay

Basement:
- 8 standard regular spaces (B-01 to B-08)
- 2 EV charging spaces (B-EV1, B-EV2) — equipped with Type 2 AC chargers (22kW)
- 2 handicapped-accessible spaces (B-H1, B-H2) — wide bays with ramp access
- Total: 12 spaces
- Surface: Underground, fully covered
- Height clearance: 2.0 meters (note: slightly lower than other levels)
- Nearest to: Elevator (central), Stairwell B, direct connection to Downtown Mall

Space Type Summary:
- Regular (standard): 43 spaces — compact and standard sizes available
- Handicapped accessible: 4 spaces — wider bays, ramped access, blue badge required
- EV Charging: 5 spaces — includes charging, no adapter needed for Type 2
- VIP Premium: 5 spaces — widest bays, best location, executive experience

Facilities on Each Level:
- Fire extinguishers and emergency exits on all levels
- CCTV cameras on all levels (100% coverage)
- Emergency call buttons at every 20 meters
- Lighting: LED, operational 24/7
- Elevator: connects all 4 levels, accessible 24/7
