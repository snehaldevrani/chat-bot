CityPark Premium Parking - Rules and Regulations

Vehicle and Driving Rules:
- Maximum speed limit inside the facility: 10 km/h at all times
- Give way to pedestrians at all times — pedestrian crossings are marked in yellow
- Follow one-way traffic flow on all levels (arrows clearly marked on floor)
- No reversing except into parking bays
- Vehicles must be switched off (engine off) while parked — no idling
- Hazard lights must be switched off once parked

Parking Space Rules:
- Park only within your designated, reserved space
- Do not park across two bays or block access lanes
- Motorcycles and bicycles are not permitted on upper levels — use the Ground Floor motorcycle bays
- Handicapped spaces are reserved exclusively for vehicles displaying a valid disability badge
- EV charging spaces are reserved exclusively for electric vehicles actively charging
- VIP spaces require a VIP reservation — unauthorized vehicles will be towed at owner's expense
- Oversize vehicles (height > 2.1m) may only use Ground Floor open-air section

Time and Reservation Rules:
- Reservations must be made at least 30 minutes in advance
- Reserved spaces are held for a maximum of 30 minutes after the reserved start time
- Unreserved spaces operate on a first-come, first-served basis
- Overnight parking (beyond closing time) is not permitted without a pre-approved monthly pass
- Vehicles remaining after closing time will be clamped and subject to a release fee

Prohibited Items and Activities:
- No storage of goods, luggage, or personal property in the facility
- No car washing, repairs, or maintenance activities inside the facility
- No smoking or use of open flames anywhere in the facility
- No hazardous materials, flammable liquids, or gas cylinders in vehicles
- No unauthorized photography or video recording of security equipment
- No loitering, solicitation, or commercial activities
- Pets must remain inside vehicles or be carried — no loose animals

Liability:
- CityPark is not responsible for theft or damage to vehicles or contents unless caused by proven negligence of our staff
- CityPark is not liable for mechanical failures occurring on the premises
- Customers park at their own risk for uninsured items left in vehicles
- CityPark management reserves the right to refuse entry or remove vehicles in violation of these rules

Lost Tickets:
- Lost parking tickets must be reported to reception immediately
- A lost ticket fee of $10 is charged before exit is permitted
- ANPR records will be used to verify entry time for lost ticket holders

Abandoned Vehicles:
- Vehicles left for more than 72 hours without authorization will be reported to authorities and may be towed
- Contact us immediately if you experience vehicle trouble to arrange extended parking
