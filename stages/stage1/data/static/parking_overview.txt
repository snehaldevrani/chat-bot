CityPark Premium Parking - Facility Overview

CityPark Premium Parking is a modern, state-of-the-art parking facility located in the heart of the Downtown Business District. Established in 2018, CityPark has become the premier parking solution for residents, daily commuters, shoppers, and visitors alike.

Our facility spans 4 levels — Ground Floor, Level 1, Level 2, and Basement — offering a total of 57 well-maintained parking spaces. We serve all types of drivers, including electric vehicle owners, VIP clients, and visitors with disabilities.

Operating Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 06:00 to 22:00
- Saturday: 07:00 to 22:00
- Sunday: 08:00 to 20:00

The facility is closed on national public holidays. Special extended hours may be available on request for corporate clients.

Key highlights of CityPark Premium Parking:
- 57 total parking spaces across 4 levels
- 5 dedicated EV charging stations
- 4 handicapped-accessible spaces
- 5 premium VIP spaces on Level 2
- 24/7 CCTV surveillance and security monitoring
- On-site security personnel during operating hours
- Clean, well-lit, and ventilated environment
- Wheelchair ramps and accessible routes on all levels
- Real-time availability tracking
- Online and in-person reservation options
- Transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees

CityPark Premium Parking is the smart choice for anyone who values safety, convenience, and quality. We are committed to providing a seamless parking experience from entry to exit.
