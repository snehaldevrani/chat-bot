CityPark Premium Parking - Features and Amenities

Security and Safety:
- 24/7 CCTV surveillance with 60+ cameras covering every bay, entrance, exit, stairwell, and elevator
- On-site security personnel present during all operating hours
- Emergency call buttons installed every 20 meters throughout the facility
- Fire detection system and automatic sprinklers on all levels
- Emergency lighting and clearly marked evacuation routes
- Controlled vehicle entry and exit via automated barrier system
- Number plate recognition (ANPR) at entry and exit points

Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging:
- 5 dedicated EV charging bays (3 on Level 1, 2 in Basement)
- Charger type: IEC 62196 Type 2 (compatible with all EU/UK EVs)
- Charging speed: 22kW AC fast charging
- Charging cable not required — cables provided at each station
- Charging included in the EV space rate ($5.00/hour)
- Real-time charger status available via chatbot

Accessibility for Disabled Visitors:
- 4 dedicated wide-bay handicapped spaces (2 on Ground, 2 in Basement)
- Wheelchair-friendly ramps on all levels
- Low-height barrier control panels at entry/exit
- Priority elevator access for visitors with disabilities
- Blue badge (disability badge) required to use handicapped spaces
- Tactile paving guides from parking spaces to elevator

Comfort and Convenience:
- Clean, well-lit facility with LED lighting on all levels
- Temperature-regulated Basement level (ventilation system)
- Free Wi-Fi throughout the facility
- Restrooms available on Ground Floor (near reception)
- Covered protection from rain and weather on all indoor levels
- Trolley/cart bays on Ground Floor for shoppers
- Lost property office at reception desk

Payment and Technology:
- Automated ticket machines on each level
- Cashless payment: credit/debit card, contactless, mobile pay
- Cash payment at Ground Floor reception desk
- Online pre-booking available via chatbot or website
- Receipts available at exit machines or on request at reception
- Monthly pass holders: dedicated fast-entry lane

VIP Experience (Level 2):
- Wider parking bays for luxury and large vehicles
- Dedicated VIP lighting and signage
- Closest spaces to the Level 2 elevator
- Priority reservation available
- Complimentary windscreen cleaning on request (weekdays)
